Welcome to BIT Bay. The Auction Site Benefitting Student IT Clubs

Everyone has stuff they no longer want or use. Now students, teachers, administrators or anyone, really, can turn that stuff into cold, hard cash that can help fund student IT clubs.

It works like an eBay™ auction, except that the sellers donate the proceeds to the IT club. Buyers compete with each via bids, with the winning bidder getting the stuff. The winning bidder then pays the IT club and the IT club makes arrangements to get the stuff to the buyer.To get started as a buyer or seller create an account. No credit card is necessary. We don't collect the funds

To get started as a buyer or seller create an account. No credit card is necessary. Just provide contact information so we can communicate with you. And, don't worry, we absolutely will not send you spam or sell your contact information to anyone.
